Leady App - Lead Generation Mobile App

My Role

UX & UI Design, Prototyping

Teams

Business Analyst, PM, Dev

Timeframe

Aug 2024 - Jan 2025

The Leady App streamlines lead generation, enhancing collaboration and efficiency. MFS Agents act as Referrers to collect leads and earn commissions for approved leads, making it engaging and rewarding.

the vision behind the leady app

Designed for a Bangladeshi company aiming to expand its customer base.

Streamlines the lead collection process as the initial touchpoint for potential clients.

Optimizes lead management to enhance efficiency.

Improves customer acquisition.

approach to design

We gathered client requirements to create a user flow, then refined the design through iterative client feedback to ensure it was functional, user-friendly, and aligned with their vision.

the journey to a seamless experience

We mapped out the user flow and refined it with client feedback to create an intuitive journey that aligned both user needs and business goals, focusing on a seamless journey for users from start to finish.

wireframe iterations, refining the blueprint

Before adding color or detail, we first drafted the app's blueprint with these low-fidelity wireframes. This allowed us to focus purely on the core structure and flow, ensuring the user's journey was logical and intuitive from the start.

bringing the blueprint to life

Home Page

This is a Leady App dashboard designed to centralize their core activities. It provides an at-a-glance view of their commission earnings, a tool for submitting and tracking customer leads, and direct access to view available loan products and promotions.

Commission Details Page

User can see commission summery by default. If they want to see specific date wise transaction then they need to tap on statement tab and they see their commissions.

Lead Status Page

Here user can see their leads status. If any lead accepted then the refferer got commission based on the accepted leads.

The Leady Mobile App transforms lead generation into an interactive and rewarding process for MFS agents. Its deliberate, user-focused design successfully optimizes lead management and accelerates customer acquisition. The result is an intuitive, scalable platform that effectively aligns business goals with user motivation to drive growth.
